ABOUT MARCOS

MARCOS is a legendary Free Fire guild created in 2017. Built on **loyalty, unity, and courage**, the guild brings players from different states together as one unstoppable force. We dominate the battlefield with precision and brotherhood.

GUILD HISTORY

MARCOS was founded in **2017 by Shikari**, a fearless leader who built the guild on teamwork and brotherhood, laying the foundation for our legacy.

The second era began under **Kaleen**, a strategic and disciplined leader supported by Kartik, Lucky, and Manas. Together, they pushed the guild into its golden age, achieving unparalleled victories.

Later, **Guddu** took charge. With the help of Ayush, Priyam, Lucky, Masoom, Momo, and Anshu, he kept the guild strong and united through various challenges.

Eventually, MARCOS went through a **downfall phase**, losing activity and members, a true test of its resilience.

Then came **Mythc (Ayush)**, an old member who returned and helped revive the guild from the ground up. His comeback brought old players together and rebuilt the MARCOS spirit, proving that legends never truly die.

Today, under the leadership of **Lucky**, MARCOS is back to its original glory — powerful, united, and respected across the battlefield.

"Once a MARCOS, Always a MARCOS."
